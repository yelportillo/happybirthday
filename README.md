# 🎂 Interactive Birthday Card

A modern, elegant, and interactive birthday experience designed to bring a smile to someone special! This isn't just a card—it's a little digital journey.

## ✨ The Experience
The card uses a "storytelling" approach. Instead of opening immediately, it builds anticipation through a series of messages on the front.

- **The Journey:** The recipient must click the card 4 times to reveal the surprise.
- **The Climax:** On the final click, the card flips open with a smooth 3D animation, gold confetti bursts across the screen, and birthday music begins to play.
- **The Design:** A professional "Midnight Navy & Gold" theme with luxury glassmorphism effects.

## 🚀 How to Use It

### For the Sender (Setup)
1. **Download/Copy the Folder:** Make sure you have the entire `birthday-card` folder.
2. **Add Your Music:** 
   - Find a birthday song you like in `.mp3` format.
   - Rename it to `music.mp3`.
   - Place it inside the `assets` folder: `birthday-card/assets/music.mp3`.
3. **Share it:** 
   - You can zip the folder and send it to your loved one.
   - Or, host it for free using **GitHub Pages** or **Netlify** so they can just click a link!

### For the Recipient (Viewing)
1. Open the `birthday-card` folder.
2. Double-click the `index.html` file.
3. Follow the prompts on the screen and keep clicking until the surprise is revealed! 🎁

## 🛠️ Technical Details
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (3D Transforms, Glassmorphism), Vanilla JavaScript.
- **Animations:** Custom CSS keyframes and `canvas-confetti` for the celebration.
- **Fonts:** Inter & Playfair Display via Google Fonts.
